Foundation
Established in 2023, the Sampurnearth Livelihood Foundation, with Tejashree Vishnu Kumbhar and Sandeep Mehto as its directors, is dedicated to creating sustainable livelihoods for sanitation workers. Our focus includes raising awareness about waste management, environmental protection, and social welfare. We aim to empower sanitation workers, enhance community dignity, and reduce landfilling by improving recycling capacities. Through various initiatives, we work to formalize the informal waste sector, set up cooperatives, and promote livelihood activities related to waste management, making a positive impact on the lives of sanitation workers and advocating for a cleaner environment.
The Green Warriors
The Green Warriors channel spotlights the vital efforts of waste workers worldwide, showcasing their daily challenges, successes, and dedication to sustainability. Through engaging content, it offers behind-the-scenes insights into waste management's significance and its impact on our planet. Subscribers gain access to narratives and discussions highlighting the critical role of waste workers in creating a cleaner, greener world. Stay updated on their stories by subscribing and tapping the notification bell. Follow #GreenWarriors for insights into sustainability and environmental heroism.
