Overview

Sampurn(e)arth, founded in 2012 by socially-driven entrepreneurs Debartha Banerjee, Ritvik Rao, and Jayanth N from TISS, is dedicated to transforming waste management for a sustainable future. Our triple bottom-line approach combines eco-friendliness, engagement with the informal sector, and economic self-sustainability. Offering decentralized solutions, we specialize in waste auditing, system design, and ongoing maintenance.

As one of India's first registered Producer Responsibility Organizations (PROs), we've been actively involved with the informal waste sector since 2012, fostering inclusivity and holistic practices. Since 2020, we have established multiple lines of plastic recycling facilities in Goa, Maharashtra and Gujarat, ensuring the transparency of the supply chain as well as social inclusion of the waste workers throughout the supply chain.

Beyond plastic, we excel in wet waste management, encompassing biogas and composting. Serving individuals, communities, and village/town/city entities, Sampurn(e)arth is your comprehensive waste management partner. Join us in creating a world where waste becomes a valuable resource, benefitting people and the planet alike.

Sampurnearth Environment Solutions Pvt. Ltd. envisions a world where waste is transformed into utilizable resources without exploitation of people or the planet. We are working towards breaking the non-cyclic process of waste management, which currently involves extraction, production, consumption and dumping or landfilling, resulting in green house gas emissions, ground water pollution and an ever-increasing strain on natural resource.

Mission
Our mission at Sampurn(e)arth is to lead the way in sustainable waste management. We are committed to creating a world where waste is no longer a problem but a resource. We work tirelessly to break the harmful cycle of waste and pollution, turning it into opportunities for communities, and ensuring the well-being of our planet. By engaging with the informal sector and promoting eco-friendly practices, we are on a journey to redefine waste management for a brighter and cleaner future.
