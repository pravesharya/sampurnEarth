EPR and Plastic Circularity Roadmap for the State of Goa
EPR and Plastic Circularity Roadmap for the State of Goa
Year :
2024
Partners :
GIZ, and Social Labs
Geography :
State of Goa, India.
This project aims to create a plastic circularity road map including the formation of an extended producer’s responsibility policy for the state of Goa.  We along with Giz are working on developing this road map which will be adopted by the Goa pollution control board and other state government agencies to form relevant policies.
Activities Performed:
  1. Orientation meetings with the key stakeholders conducting multi stakeholder surveys.

  2. Receipt and analysis of secondary data

  3. Review of global and national best practices for roadmap development

  4. Organizing a Conference on Circular Plastic Waste Management, Goa

  5. Preparation for policy notification and roadmap for the state
