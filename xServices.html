Post Consumer Recyclate (PCR)
Post-consumer recyclates are materials that have been used by consumers and then collected for recycling, turning what would have been waste into valuable resources for new products. This process not only conserves natural resources but also reduces greenhouse gas emissions and energy consumption compared to producing goods from virgin materials. By incorporating post-consumer recyclates into manufacturing, businesses can significantly lessen their environmental impact while supporting a circular economy. With the new compliance rules for PCR coming into effect, we work actively with brands and producers to meet the same.

Dry Waste Management (DWM Services)
Sampurnearth specializes in efficient dry waste management solutions. We collect, segregate, and recycle dry waste, ensuring minimal environmental impact. Our comprehensive approach addresses various dry waste materials, contributing to a cleaner, greener future aiming to end landfilling of our post-consumer dry waste.
